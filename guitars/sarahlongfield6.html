Strandberg Sarah Longfield Standard 6

Standberg Sarah Longfield Standard 6

The Strandberg Sarah Longfield Standard 6 is a recent purchase, made in 2022. It has quickly become my number one guitar, outpacing even technically better units such as the Strandberg Original 6 and the Standberg OS7.

Pros

  • Playability and setup is done perfectly
  • Looks great
  • Stringjoy 9.5s customs are a joy to play and bend with, with the pickups
  • The Polymaths are top notch - sweet, singing neck

Cons

  • Satin finish will buff
  • Bridge humbucker has less output, sounds less clinical than the BKP BHs, however you don't need that much higher output when paired with the Omega Granophyre
  • Unchamberd body means that it is heavier than the flagship models
